Signing Procedure

Generally, I will meet with another user in reasonable coniditons, and verify his or her identity against a government-issued photo ID. At the time of signing, the user should present me with a written record of the key fingerprint and UIDs to be signed. I'll then signed the signed public key to the keyholder only, to distribute how he or she seems fit. My signature for each UID will be sent to that UID only, confirming that the key owner has access to that UID. I'll also sign UIDs containing photos, assuming the photo reasonably matches the identity of the key owner, and will send it to the primary UID on the provided key.
